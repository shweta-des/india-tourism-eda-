# india-tourism-eda-
Exploratory Data Analysis of India Tourism (2001-2023) using Pandas &amp; Plotly

> Exploratory Data Analysis of 23 years of Indian tourism statistics, 
> uncovering stories of resilience, growth, and geographic shifts.

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes **23 years** (2001-2023) of India's tourism data to understand:
- Long-term growth trends and patterns
- COVID-19 impact and recovery dynamics
- Geographic distribution and market shifts
- Economic contribution (Foreign Exchange Earnings)

**Key Finding:** Tourism in India fell sharply in 2020 due to COVID-19(65% crash), but recovered strongly and surpassed pre-pandemic levels by 2023. By then, total arrivals were 6% higher than in 2019, with Bangladesh becoming the largest source of foreign tourists.

---

## Key Insights

### 1. The Resilience Story
- **2001-2019:** Steady growth from 2.5M to 17.9M tourists
- **2020:** 65% decline due to COVID-19
- **2023:** Recovered to 18.9M (106% of 2019 levels)

### 2. The Quality Story
- India ranks **14th globally** in tourism earnings
- But ranks **24th** in tourist arrivals
- Foreign Exchange Earnings increased nearly 10Ã—, rising from $3.2B to $32.2B

### 3. The Geographic Shift
- **Bangladesh** is now the #1 source market (2.1M tourists, 22.3%)
- **South Asia** collectively contributes 29% of arrivals
- Traditional markets (USA, UK) remain strong but relatively declining
